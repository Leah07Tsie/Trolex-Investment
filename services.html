Application for Permits

This includes applications for permanent residence, emergency work permits, short-term, and long-term permits.

Citizenship: This is for people who have resided in Botswana for the past ten years with no break. For example, having ten residential and work permits for the previous years and would like to become a citizen.

Short Term Permits: Commonly known as emergency work permits, they range from:

  • One (1) day to fourteen days
  • Fourteen days to three months

Long Term Permits: This permit duration is from four (4) months to five (5) years.
